Interior Design Process

We transform the design dreams of our clients into a full-fledged reality with space planning and furniture customisations perfectly aligned to your unique taste. Here is a glimpse of our workflow.

1

Online Consultation

We begin with a virtual meet to understand your vision, preferences, and space needs—convenient and commitment-free.

2

Approximate Quotation

Based on your inputs, we share an estimated cost to give you clarity and help you plan better.

3

In Person Discussion

We dive deeper into your project with an on-site or face-to-face discussion to refine ideas and expectations.

4

Advance Booking

Secure your project slot with us by making a token advance payment—your dream transformation begins here.

5

2D/3D Design

We craft personalized 2D layouts and 3D visualizations so you can preview your space before execution.

6

Material Presentation

We showcase handpicked materials, finishes, and textures tailored to your design and budget.

7

Final Quotation

After locking the design and materials, we present a detailed final quotation—transparent and all-inclusive.

8

Execution Begins

Our expert team gets to work, ensuring timely progress with premium quality and precision.

9

Project Handover

We deliver your completed space with a final walk-through, quality check, and client satisfaction at heart.
